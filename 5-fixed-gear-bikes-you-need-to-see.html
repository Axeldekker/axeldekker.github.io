5 high quality bikes you can’t miss out on this spring

by Lars van Ruitenbeek 28 March 2018

The days are getting warmer and the sun is staying out longer. This means cycling season has started! Of course, as a cycling enthusiast weather doesn’t slow you down, however your afternoon ride is a bit more enjoyable when you are accompanied by the sun. To get you ready for this spring we picked 5 of our top favorites when considering quality and appearance.

Starting of straight away with our absolute favorite, the still to be released Aventon Mataro 2018 edition. This newly updated Mataro will be released by the end of April and promises to live up its reputation. It comes with a carbon seatpost, which means its lightweight, strong and looks sick. Besides, the updated tapered headtube makes it possible for you to whip your bike around corners without fear. Overall they did a great job in improving the design of this bike. You gotta agree with us on this one, it’s an absolute beauty.

More into a bike that fits your everyday city ride? Perhaps the State Bicycle Co. Black Label V2 is just the right thing for you. Provided with the option to either pick a drop- or riser bar, both road and track are yours to dominate on this beast. Choose for a riser bar with Vans grips and zigzag around cars and pedestrians while cruising through urban areas. Because of the ultralight aluminum frame and carbon forefork it weighs in at only 8 kg. Of course, just as important, the smooth design makes it a real eye-catcher.

Moving on with the list we wanted to include a retro looking model in here as well. The Quella Premium Varsity collection has been around since Quella started manufacturing bikes, so it’s nothing new, however still definitely worth a mention. The vintage-inspired paint finish and hand-made leather saddle and bar tape creates the signature Quella aesthetic. These details combined with the 40mm Deep-V rims and durable hi-tensile steel frame ensure a relaxed ride when you’re into urban cycling. The exact opposite of what’s next on our list…

The Unknown Bikes Singularity. Unknown Bikes and a relaxed ride? Hell no! The Singularity incorporates a modern tube set, an update geometry and a short wheelbase to be the ultimate race machine. This track oriented frame includes an integrated aero seatpost and carbon/alloy fork along with other quality components. What else we need to say? If you are into racing your fixed gear bike, this bike suits you right.

We end this list with (of course) another bike that guarantees to turn heads. The Pure Fix Premium Series come with a chrome fork and polished silver quill stem and seat which brings a dash of classic style to them. Another retro design? Yes, we simply love those models! But, don’t worry about your pace, the Premium Series was bred to be lighter, stiffer, and faster.
